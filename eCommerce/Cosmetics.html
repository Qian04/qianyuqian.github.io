About Us

Chih Beauty is a new Malaysian cosmetics brand that sells a wide range of skincare and cosmetics. We also have online purchase channels on all major platforms, and we have opened offline experience stores in several malls and shops. In addition, the brand adopts a membership sales and promotion method, which has increased a lot of revenue and customer sources for the brand.

Business Model Key Points

Value Proposition

Chih Beauty sells cosmetics, supplemented by skin care products and perfumes. The main purpose of Chih Beauty is to make customers who use the brand aware that they are always beautiful and to help them feel confident. In addition, we also provide sales services for high and low consumers, and also provide cosmetic products of different materials for consumers with different skin types.

Revenue Model

Chih Beauty has multiple revenue models at the same time. This allows the brand to maintain profitability for a long time and develop more new products and promotional plans. First is product sales, we earn income by selling several cosmetic products. Second is membership system, only fixed consumption customers can become members. Members can buy items at better prices on Member days, and they can also exchange some items with brands by earning points. Last, we also provide makeup services, hiring professional stylists to dress customers for various types of banquets or ceremonies. This requires the customer to pay extra and make an appointment.

Market Opportunity

Cosmetics target a wide range of people, age and gender are not required, but the majority of consumers are usually between 20 and 40 years old women. Other than women, our brand also reaches the male market with products such as skin care, makeup, and perfume suitable for men. Besides that, compared with traditional cosmetics, natural and organic cosmetics will be more friendly to human skin. It avoids harmful chemicals such as synthetic ingredients and reduces the risk of allergies.

Organizational Development

What a mature company needs most is employees who can cooperate and perform their own duties. Each necessary process requires a dedicated department.

The company's departments can be divided into product design, sales, publicity and quality assurance Departments and others. However, these four departments are the most important, they are interlinked to design the products that are beneficial to the brand and customers.
