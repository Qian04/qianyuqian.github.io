Curry Chicken

  • 800g chicken
  • 1/2-1 coconut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 large onion
  • 3 stalks curry leaves


  1. Saute curry paste in hot oil until fragrant. Add in chicken.
    Continue to saute untilchicken release oil.

  2. Second, add in water, tamarind paste, curry leaves, potato, onion, sugar and salt.
    Let chicken simmer in slow fire untill potatoes becomes soft and chicken is cooked throughly.

  3. Add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to seperate.
    Remove and serve.
